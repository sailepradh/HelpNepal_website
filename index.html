Help Nepal Network-Sweden

HeNN-Sweden, one of the fourteen chapter of Help Nepal Network (HeNN) was registered as a non-profit organization in sweden in 2006 . The organization number (802432-3340) in this regard was issued by national Tax Board of Sweden on 30th May 2006.

HeNN Sweden's Major Activities

Terai Flood Victims Donation

Southern part of Nepal has been badly affected by the flood in August 2017 and HeNN Sweden helped with amount 12000 SEK to Nepal Yuva which actively participated to help the victims in sanitation mainly to the school kids in different school.

Dudhpokhari Primary School

HeNN Sweden contributed for the construction of Dudhkoshi Primary School, which had been destroyed by Earthquake in 2015.

Health Post Mugu

HELP NEPAL Network constructed the health post at a remote village in one of the most far-flung d istricts of Nepal in 2004. The total cost of the project came to around US$ 14,000 of which 50% came as an in-kind support from the local community. This included the land, building material and labour costs. Day to day running of the center is supervised by the village committee with monthly reports submitted to both local governmnet as well as HeNN. Operating cost of the healthpost is taken care by HeNN.
