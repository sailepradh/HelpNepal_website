About Us

HeNN SWEDEN Help Nepal Network(HeNN) is a global charity with chapters in 14 countries. The aim of the Network is to encourage Nepalis and those who love Nepal around the world to provide assistance in the fields of health, education in rural Nepal. The Network also provides support to disaster relief efforts. HELP NEPAL Network Nepal, based in Kathmandu, co-ordinates and supervises the Network's activities and projects in Nepal.

HeNN-Sweden, one of the fourteen chapter of Help Nepal Network (HeNN) was registered as a non-profit organization in sweden in 2006 . The organization number (802432-3340) in this regard was issued by national Tax Board of Sweden on 30th May 2006.

HeNN Sweden aims to work in a transparent and responsible manner , to collect funds on voluntary basis to provide aid in health, education , development of infrastructure and in disaster relief efforts in Nepal. The means of collecting money is through membership fee, donations, organising social and fund raising activities in Sweden.

HeNN has completed more than 100 projects in Nepal in health, education and disaster relief. Out of those, HeNN Sweden has contributed in following projects in Nepal.
